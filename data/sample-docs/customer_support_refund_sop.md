# EuroFlow Payments Group customer support and reimbursement SOP

Customer support handles payment questions, failed transfer reports, suspected APP fraud, unauthorised payment reports, merchant disputes and account access issues. Support agents can view payment status, transaction ID, beneficiary IBAN, execution timestamp, fee disclosure, fraud review status and warning events shown to the customer.

For suspected fraud, support creates a fraud ticket and tags it as payment fraud, account takeover, impersonation, merchant dispute or unknown. The fraud operations team reviews tagged cases and can request more customer information.

If a customer reports an unauthorised payment, support freezes the wallet when account takeover is suspected and escalates the ticket to fraud operations. The customer receives an acknowledgement email with ticket reference and expected first response time.

For authorised push payment fraud, support records the customer's narrative, impersonation indicators and any screenshots supplied by the customer. The SOP does not yet include a complete APP fraud liability decision tree, reimbursement owner or final customer outcome template.

Support keeps notes, uploaded customer screenshots, internal decisions and customer notices on the ticket. Severe cases can be escalated to compliance leadership for additional review.
